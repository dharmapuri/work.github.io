About LPU Stamp World

Lovely Professional University (LPU) is a private university situated in Phagwara, Punjab, India. The university was established in 2005.


It is the largest single campus university in India (including Govt. and Private Sector), with more than 30,000 students from across the world, 4000 faculty and staff.


It conducts more than one extra curriculum activities to improve students' performance. Among them is 'Stamp Collection'.
